Biography

My name is Julia Nelson. I have brown curly hair and green eyes. I grew up in a small town in New Jersey with three other siblings and my parents. By my sophomore year of high school, I was recruited to play lacrosse at Franklin & Marshall College, where I attended my first year of higher education.

Originally wanting to be a Mechanical Engineer, F&M was a great school for me. However, after trying out a Computer Science class and loving it, I realized F&M was not the right place to study that. After making the decision to leave F&M and my lacrosse team, I spent a semester applying to different colleges. Hearing about Stevens Institute of Technology's Computer Science program and the campus' relatively close location to my hometown, I transferred to continue my education.
